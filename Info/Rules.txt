The following acts are considered illegal throughout much of the land:

- Murder
- Theft from Others
- Grave Robbing
- Carving Human Corpses
- Being a Death Knight
- Being a Syth

These acts are considered unfavorable in the eyes of the civilized:

- Begging
- Necromancy
- Eating the Hearts of Humans
- Slaying Good Natured Creatures
- Dealing in Poisons
